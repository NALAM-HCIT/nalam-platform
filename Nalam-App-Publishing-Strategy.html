Nalam — Hospital App Publishing Strategy

Single App vs Per-Hospital Apps

Management Review · 29 March 2026

The Question

When we onboard 100 hospitals, how should we publish the app?

🅰 Option A — One App

  • One "Nalam Health" app
  • Patient selects hospital inside app
  • One Play Store listing

🅱 Option B — Per Hospital

  • Each hospital gets their own app
  • Their name & logo on Play Store
  • 100 Play Store listings

🅰 Option A — How It Works

One shared app for all hospitals

Patient opens Play Store
↓
Searches "Nalam Health"
↓
Downloads one generic app
↓
Selects hospital inside the app
↓
Logs in to their hospital

🅱 Option B — How It Works

Each hospital has their own branded app

Patient opens Play Store
↓
Searches "Arun Priya Hospital"
↓
Finds their hospital's own app
↓
Downloads & logs in directly

We add a new hospital
↓
Add hospital config + logo (5 min)
↓
Run automated build script (30 min)
↓
Upload to Play Store → Live in 1-2 days

🎯 The Deciding Question

When a hospital pays monthly subscription, what are they paying for?

OPTION A TELLS PATIENTS:
"Download the 'Nalam Health' app from Play Store, then select our hospital."
OPTION B TELLS PATIENTS:
"Search 'Arun Priya Hospital' on Play Store and download our app."
Option B is simpler for patients and more prestigious for the hospital.

In the healthcare B2B market, hospitals are brand-conscious. A patient seeing "Arun Priya Hospital" on their phone homescreen builds trust. A patient seeing "Nalam Health" and selecting inside feels like a shared platform — not their hospital's own app.

Hospitals paying a monthly subscription expect their brand — not ours.

📱 iPhone (iOS) Reality

Apple does not allow 100 separately branded apps from one account

Platform 🅰 One App 🅱 Per Hospital India Market Share
Android ✓ Works ✓ Works 95%
iPhone ✓ Works ✗ Apple rejects 5%

⚠️ iPhone is not a priority for India right now. We handle iOS with Option A (one generic app) when the need arises. Android-first is the correct strategy.

✅ Recommendation

PHASE 1 — NOW (0 to 50 hospitals)

Android: Option B — Per-Hospital Branded Apps Android

Each hospital gets their name and logo on Play Store. Automated build system — adding a new hospital takes 30 minutes. One codebase maintained by our team. Strongest selling point when pitching to new hospitals.

PHASE 2 — LATER (50+ hospitals or when iOS is needed)

iPhone: Option A — One "Nalam Health" App iOS

Hospitals accept the generic name for iPhone given Apple's restrictions. India is 95% Android so this affects very few patients.
